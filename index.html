Stephen King

Professional headshot of King

Stephen King is an American horror writer from Bangor, Maine. He is the author of more than 100 books, including It, Pet Sematary, The Institute, The Shining, Misery, and more. He is married to Tabitha King and is the father of writers Joe Hill and Owen King, with whom he has cowritten some books. Many of King's works have been adapted for film and television, including It, The Shining, Doctor Sleep, Pet Sematary, and Cujo, to name a few.

For more information about Stephen King, please check out his Wikipedia page.
